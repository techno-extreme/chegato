School Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs).




This section contains a wealth of information, related to Chegato High School. If you cannot find an answer to your question, make sure to contact us.

General questions

What curriculum does the school follow?

We follow an adapted version of the ZIMSEC Syllabus,STEM and the International General Certificate of Secondary Education (IGCSE).

What were the previous O and A Level reults?

O LEVEL RESULTS- 44.4%
A LEVEL RESULTS- 72.6%

What is the teacher-student ratio in the school?

Our average student-teacher ratio is :
40:1

Does the school have boarding(residential)facilities?

Yes the school offers both non-residents and boarding residents.
Parents

Does the school have filtered internet access?

Yes, by law the school uses a filter for the internet.

How to find out how much my child is owing to the school?

To check student balance, have to login into the student portal.

Who do i talk to concerning my child's performance?

To follow progress of the student, parents can communicate directly with the student teacher by loging in into the student portal and use direct messaging.

How to check student's grades?

To check student results, login into the student portal.

What documentation do i need to enroll a new student?

You will need a county birth certificate, copy of immunization records, and proof of residency (any documentation showing your name and current address).

How do you ensure safety and healthy of students?

Safety and healthy of students is our top priority , there is a small dispensary at school premises and a van to feary students to nearby hospital for complicated cases, as for safety there is a safety recovery plan.
Students

Does the school offer afterschool activities?

Yes there are different afterschool activities for more information contact the admin.

Where can i find school timetable?

You can find the current term school timetable on our School Home Page.

What do i if i have a concern regarding a teacher?

Please begin by speaking directly to the teacher. If the issue is not resolved, contact the superiors of the teacher ie HOD, Headmaster.

How to access school online resources?

Online resources are accessed by login into the student portal.

Where can i find school calender?

You can find the current year school calendar on our School Home Page.
